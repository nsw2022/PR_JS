Frequently Asked Questions

What is RabenRifaie?

We are a creative design studio. Branding and 3D design experts supporting our clients in all design-related needs, all through the spirit of storytelling and building long-lasting relationships.

What do you do?

We are experts in the following domains:
- Branding and all graphic design-related activities
- 3D design from product visualizations to complex animations
- Motion design and 2D animation
- Storytelling and copywriting

I need a project, what now?

Get in touch with us! If you need one project, get in touch with us through email or book an e-coffee to talk face-to-face about details. After the first call, we will create a quote for your review. If you need more than one project, consider Design Partnership and definitely schedule an e-coffee to chat more.

What is Design Partnership?

Think about it like a membership or design subscription service. Do you represent a company that needs a certain amount of projects designed regularly? Done! For a fixed sum a month, we will deliver an agreed number of projects.

What is included in the Design Partnership?

Every Design Partnership agreement is tailored individually to each client’s needs. Do you need support with only graphic design? Done. Are you more interested in 3D animations? Let’s chat. Or perhaps you regularly need help with a number of design projects that fall into various design domains? Let’s talk about it. Our aim is to cater to all your design needs.

Where are you located?

The studio is based in Poznan, Poland. However, we work remotely with clients from all over the world.

How much do you charge?

We quote each client individually after getting to know the design needs and the brief a little bit closer. We try to present you with an estimate quote after our first call.
